CCA Treated Poles & Timber

Our product are treated with Level H4 CCA (Copper Chrome Arsenate), which is a wood preservation method designed to protect against decay and insect damage. This treatment is suitable for outdoor applications, ensuring durability and longevity in various environmental conditions.

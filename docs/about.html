Our Story

Founded in 2020,just after the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, our business
began on a small space at a road testing station. We were fortunate to be
surrouned by incredible people who supported us through those early,
uncertain days.

With hard work, determintation, and gratitude to the Lord, we overcame many
challenges and expanded into a larger yard. This growth has allowed us to offer
a wider range of products and better serve our customers.

What we Offer

We specialize in high-quality poles and timber. All our products are:

  • SABS Approved
  • H4 CCA Treated for durabiity and long-term reliability

Our product range includes:

  • Gum Poles
  • Timber
  • Ridging
  • Water Tanks
  • Compost

We're committed to meeting the needs of builders, landscapers, farmers, and
DIYers across the region.

Moving Forward

Despite economic challenges, we continue to grow and improve each year.
Our faith keeps us grounded, and we trust that our doors will always remain open
to serve our valued customers.

Thank You

To our loyal customers - thank you. Your support and trust have been the
foundation of our success. We look forward to continuing to serve you with
